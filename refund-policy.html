Refund Policy

Return and Exchange Policy

Ready-to-Wear Items

We offer a 14-day return policy for ready-to-wear items. This means you have 14 days after receiving your item to request a return or exchange. To be eligible for a return, your item must be in the same condition as when you received it — unworn or unused, with tags, and in its original packaging. You'll also need the order confirmation or proof of purchase.

Non-Returnable Items

Please note that we do not accept returns or exchanges on the following items:

  • Custom-made products
  • Alterations on ready-to-wear items
  • Gift cards

If you have questions or concerns about a specific item, please contact us at orders@nurael.co.

Sale Items

Sale items are eligible for a refund within 3 days of receipt, provided they are in their original packaging and have not been altered in any way.

How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, please reach out to us at orders@nurael.co. Once your return is approved, we will provide you with return instructions and, where applicable, a shipping label for arranging a pickup.

For any return questions, feel free to contact us at orders@nurael.co or via our contact page.

Damages and Issues

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, please notify us via email at orders@nurael.co within 48 hours of receipt. To help us assess the issue promptly, please attach photos of the item, including close-up photos of any damage or fault, along with your order reference.

Refunds

Once we've received and inspected your return, we'll let you know if the refund is approved. If approved, you'll be automatically refunded to your original payment method within 5–10 business days. Please remember it can take some time for your bank or card issuer to process and post the refund.
